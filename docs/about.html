About Us!


We - Chloe, Sara and Mercy - are students from George Mason University

The Problem We Sought to Fix:

People with food allergies often find themselves needing to communicate their allergens to other people and neediing to make substitutions in recipes. In order to make it easier for people with food allergies to find suitable recipes and share their allergens with their friends, we decided to create a website that allows users to add their allergies to their profile, share this profile with friends, and search common recipe websites for recipes. Upon searching for a recipe, the user will recieve a list of recipes that have automatically been edited with notes to make any necessary substitutions that accommodate the person's allergies.
